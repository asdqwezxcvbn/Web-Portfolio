MechTrack

MechTrack is a app in which i made for my Final in my first semester at Ranken Technical College, MechTrack as the name suggests, is a app in which is deployed on your device locally saving your data which tracks updates and assigns mechanics jobs based on their work week hours and the average time it takes to complete each task. MechTrack was created as a app to fix the scheduling and time management problems for the mechanics at my dads work. Here they had trouble correctly organizing and assigning new customer jobs due to un expected required repairs, using an old fashioned system where orders where written down on paper and delivered to mechanics, amount of customer vehicles needed to be worked on,certain specifications needed to be able to even undo a bolt on some car models, and no system to even have record of who has what job and how many hours each person has worked each week other then the amount of job slips they turn in.
